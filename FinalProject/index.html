Frequently Asked Questions

+ What services does your spa offer?

At SPA Luxe, we offer a wide range of spa services, including massages, facials, body treatments, manicures, pedicures, and more. Our menu is designed to cater to various wellness needs, providing a comprehensive and luxurious spa experience.

+ How do I make a reservation?

Booking a spa experience at SPA Luxe is easy! You can make a reservation by calling our front desk, visiting our website, or using our convenient online booking system. We recommend booking in advance to ensure availability.

+ Are gift certificates available?

Yes, we offer gift certificates for our spa services. They make perfect gifts for special occasions or a thoughtful gesture for a loved one. Gift certificates can be purchased online or in-person at our spa.

+ What should I wear to my spa appointment?

We recommend wearing comfortable clothing to your spa appointment. Upon arrival, you will be provided with a robe and slippers to wear during your treatments. Our therapists are trained to ensure your comfort and modesty at all times.

+ Can I choose a specific therapist for my treatment?

Yes, you can request a specific therapist when making your reservation, and we will do our best to accommodate your preference. All our therapists are highly skilled and trained to provide exceptional service.
